What does subtraction mean?

Here you will learn about subtraction.

To successfully perform the task of subtracting numbers, you must know what subtraction does. The term "subtraction" represents a mathematical operation. Subtraction is an action that you perform using numbers. Subtraction allows you to find the difference between 2 numbers.

You use the minus (-) button to express the subtraction operation in the app.

In the subtraction process, you take a number and reduce it to a smaller number. That is, you take away one number from another.
